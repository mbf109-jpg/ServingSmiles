About Our Partner Beneficiary

The Marist School Marikina Canteen contains food stall concessionares who often work long hours and earn minimum wages while being underappreciated.
Which is why we established the "Serving Smiles!" canteen outreach program which aims to make each and every one of our everyday private chefs feel appreciated!

About "Serving Smiles!"

"Serving Smiles!" LLC was established by Prince Calcita, Melch Castillo, Jaime Caro, Wilhelm Elaurza, and Amiel Dela Cruz as a joint partnership aimed at supporting the young and the old alike. With our shared love of Jesus and His Gospel, we are motivated everyday to work and strive to better our community in any way, shape or form.

Motivated to unite as one nation fighting for the same cause

"Serving smiles for everyone at each and every mile!"
