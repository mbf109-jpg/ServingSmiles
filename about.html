Welcome to Our Community Outreach Program!

As steadfast Maristas following in the way of Mary, Our Good Mother,
and St. Marcellin Champagnat, we are committed to taking care of our
past, engaging in the present, and fostering the future. In line
with all of this, Serving Smiles! LLC was initiated to make sure
that the elders of our past and the children of the future are taken
care of properly in the love that Jesus Christ taught us. Together,
let us all follow in the way that both Jesus and Mary has laid down for us!

About Us

Our Outreach Society as a whole is dedicated to the proposition that all
men are created equal. As old or as young as you are, we are all deserving
of the same love and care that Jesus Christ had shown for us. In line with this,
we have several programs that simultaneously care for the past and foster the
future of our society. Our primary program, the Canteen Worker Appreciation Day, is an
opportunity for most to get involved in our activities.

The "Serving Smiles!" Appreciation Day!

More About Us

"Serving Smiles!" LLC was established by Prince Calcita, Melch Castillo,
Jaime Caro, Wilhelm Elaurza, and Amiel Dela Cruz as a joint partnership
aimed at supporting the young and the old alike. With our shared love of
Jesus and His Gospel, we are motivated everyday to work and strive to
better our community in any way, shape or form.
